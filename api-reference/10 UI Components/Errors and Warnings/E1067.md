##### shortDescription
Occurs in [DataGrid](/Documentation/Guide/UI_Components/DataGrid/Overview/) and [TreeList](/Documentation/Guide/UI_Components/TreeList/Overview/) when **aiIntegration** is not configured.

---
To resolve this error, specify [aiIntegration](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxDataGrid/Configuration/#aiIntegration) in the component or AI column configuration.
