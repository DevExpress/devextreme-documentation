##### shortDescription
Aligns axis labels in relation to ticks.

---
<!-- Description goes here -->

The following table represents how this property aligns the labels of horizontal/vertical axes:

<table class="dx-table">
    <tr>
        <th>Value</th>
        <th>Horizontal Axes</th>
        <th>Vertical Axes</th>
    </tr>        
    <tr>
        <td><code>center</code></td>
        <td>Centers labels to their corresponding ticks.</td>
        <td>Applies central text alignment.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td><code>left</code></td>
        <td>Aligns the left edges of labels to their corresponding ticks</td>
        <td>Applies left text alignment.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td><code>right</code></td>
        <td>Aligns the right edges of labels to their corresponding ticks</td>
        <td>Applies right text alignment.</td>
    </tr>
</table>
