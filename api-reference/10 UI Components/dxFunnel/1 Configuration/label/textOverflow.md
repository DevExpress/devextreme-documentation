##### shortDescription
Specifies what to do with label texts that overflow the allocated space after applying [wordWrap](/api-reference/20%20Data%20Visualization%20Widgets/dxFunnel/1%20Configuration/label/wordWrap.md '/Documentation/ApiReference/Data_Visualization_Widgets/dxFunnel/Configuration/label/#wordWrap'): hide, truncate them and display an ellipsis, or do nothing.
