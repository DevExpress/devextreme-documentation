---
id: dxSlideOut.toggleMenuVisibility(showing)
---
---
##### shortDescription
Shows or hides the slide-out menu depending on the argument.

##### return: Promise<void>
A Promise that is resolved after the menu is shown/hidden.
#include ref-promisedistinction

##### param(showing): Boolean | undefined
Specifies whether to show or hide the UI component. **undefined** reverses the current visibility state.

---
You can use this method instead of the [showMenu()](/api-reference/10%20UI%20Components/dxSlideOut/3%20Methods/showMenu().md '{basewidgetpath}/Methods/#showMenu') and [hideMenu()](/api-reference/10%20UI%20Components/dxSlideOut/3%20Methods/hideMenu().md '{basewidgetpath}/Methods/#hideMenu') methods.
