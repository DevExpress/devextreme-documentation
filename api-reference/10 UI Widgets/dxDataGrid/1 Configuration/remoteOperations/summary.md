##### shortDescription
Specifies whether or not [summaries](/concepts/05%20Widgets/DataGrid/65%20Summaries/10%20Total%20Summary '/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/DataGrid/Summaries/Total_Summary/') summaries are calculated on the server-side.

---
[note]When summary calculation is remote, [custom client-side summaries](/concepts/05%20Widgets/DataGrid/65%20Summaries/07%20Custom%20Aggregate%20Function/10%20Client-Side%20Data%20Aggregation.md '/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/DataGrid/Summaries/Custom_Aggregate_Function/#Client-Side_Data_Aggregation') are not supported.
