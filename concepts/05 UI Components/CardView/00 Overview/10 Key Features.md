- **Data Binding**    
CardView supports loading and updating data from different [data source](/Documentation/ApiReference/Data_Layer/DataSource/) types. It handles large data sets efficiently with both local data stores and [remote data operations](https://js.devexpress.com/Demos/WidgetsGallery/Demo/CardView/WebAPIService/).

- **Editing Operations**    
CardView can allow users to add, update, and delete cards. You can configure which operations are accessible to users by defining [allowAdding](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/editing/#allowAdding), [allowDeleting](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/editing/#allowDeleting), and [allowUpdating](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/editing/#allowUpdating) in the [editing](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/editing/) object.

- **Filtering and Sorting**    
Configure [headerFilter](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/headerFilter/) or [filterPanel](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/filterPanel/) to allow users to filter CardView data. Modify sorting settings in the [sorting](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/sorting/) object.

- **Card Customization**    
You can customize the appearance of cards with a variety of objects, including the following:
    - [cardCover](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/cardCover/)
    - [cardHeader](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/cardHeader/)
    - [cardFooterTemplate](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/#cardFooterTemplate)
    - [cardContentTemplate](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/#cardContentTemplate)
    - [columns](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/columns/).[fieldTemplate](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/columns/#fieldTemplate)

    Generate fully custom cards with [cardTemplate](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/#cardTemplate).

    - [paging](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/paging/)
    - [selection](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/selection/)
    - [cardsPerRow](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxCardView/Configuration/#cardsPerRow)
