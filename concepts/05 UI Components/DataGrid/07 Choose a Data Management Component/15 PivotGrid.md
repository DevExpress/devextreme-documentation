Displays and analyzes multidimensional data. Used for OLAP reporting and analytics. Supports custom aggregation. For an expanded list of features, see [PivotGrid Key Features](/concepts/05%20UI%20Components/PivotGrid/02%20Overview/10%20Key%20Features.md '/Documentation/Guide/UI_Components/PivotGrid/Overview/#Key_Features').

<img src="/images/pivotgrid/pivot-grid-choose.png" alt="DevExtreme PivotGrid" />

### Typical Usage Scenarios

- Sales analysis by region, period, or product  
- Comparison of financial metrics  
- Presenting information in tabular form and as charts 

[note] Use the [PivotGrid](/concepts/05%20UI%20Components/PivotGrid/02%20Overview/00%20Overview.md '/Documentation/Guide/UI_Components/PivotGrid/Overview/') for multidimensional datasets requiring flexible aggregation, analysis, and pivoting.
