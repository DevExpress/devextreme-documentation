- [exportColumnFieldHeaders](/ApiReference/Common/Object_Structures/PivotGridExportOptions/#exportColumnFieldHeaders)
- [exportDataFieldHeaders](/api-reference/50%20Common/Object%20Structures/PivotGridExportOptions/exportDataFieldHeaders.md '/Documentation/ApiReference/Common/Object_Structures/PivotGridExportOptions/#exportDataFieldHeaders')
- [exportFilterFieldHeaders](/api-reference/50%20Common/Object%20Structures/PivotGridExportOptions/exportFilterFieldHeaders.md '/Documentation/ApiReference/Common/Object_Structures/PivotGridExportOptions/#exportFilterFieldHeaders')
- [exportRowFieldHeaders](/api-reference/50%20Common/Object%20Structures/PivotGridExportOptions/exportRowFieldHeaders.md '/Documentation/ApiReference/Common/Object_Structures/PivotGridExportOptions/#exportRowFieldHeaders')
