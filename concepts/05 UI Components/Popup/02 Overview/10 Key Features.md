- The Popup component is highly customizable. You can define its [content](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxPopup/Configuration/#contentTemplate), [header](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxPopup/Configuration/#titleTemplate), and [footer](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxPopup/Configuration/toolbarItems/).

- [Position and align](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxPopup/Configuration/#position) Popup relative to the viewport or a specific element.

- Configure opening and closing [animations](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxPopup/Configuration/animation/). The [settings](/Documentation/ApiReference/Common/Object_Structures/AnimationConfig/) include duration, delay, type, and so on.

- Choose how users can close the Popup: [show the close button](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxPopover/Configuration/#showCloseButton), [hide on the outside click](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Components/dxPopover/Configuration/#hideOnOutsideClick), or [define your custom close button](https://js.devexpress.com/Demos/WidgetsGallery/Demo/Popup/Overview/).
