Total Rows and Total Columns display sub-totals calculated for outer row/column fields. For example, if you expand the UK group, you will see the rows characterizing cities and also a **UK Total** row. This row is equal to the collapsed **UK** row.

Total rows can be enabled or disabled with the [showColumnTotals](/api-reference/10%20UI%20Widgets/dxPivotGrid/1%20Configuration/showColumnTotals.md '/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxPivotGrid/Configuration/#showColumnTotals') option, and total columns &#8212; the [showRowTotals](/api-reference/10%20UI%20Widgets/dxPivotGrid/1%20Configuration/showRowTotals.md '/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxPivotGrid/Configuration/#showRowTotals') option. You can also change the location of total rows or columns using the [showTotalsPrior](/api-reference/10%20UI%20Widgets/dxPivotGrid/1%20Configuration/showTotalsPrior.md '/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxPivotGrid/Configuration/#showTotalsPrior') option.
