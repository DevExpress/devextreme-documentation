DevExtreme comes with an icon library that contains SVG and font icons for all DevExtreme themes. You can use the icons [in widgets](/concepts/60%20Themes%20and%20Styles/30%20Icons/20%20Icons%20in%20Widgets.md '/Documentation/Guide/Themes_and_Styles/Icons/#Icons_in_Widgets') and [in other page elements](/concepts/60%20Themes%20and%20Styles/30%20Icons/30%20Icons%20in%20Other%20HTML%20Elements.md '/Documentation/Guide/Themes_and_Styles/Icons/#Icons_in_Other_HTML_Elements') as they are or [customize them](/concepts/60%20Themes%20and%20Styles/30%20Icons/35%20Customize%20Icons.md '/Documentation/Guide/Themes_and_Styles/Icons/#Customize_Icons').
