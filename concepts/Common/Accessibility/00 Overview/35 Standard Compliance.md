We aim to align our components with the following standards:

- [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)](https://www.w3.org/WAI/standards-guidelines/wcag/)
- [Section 508](https://www.access-board.gov/ict/)
- [European Accessibility Act (EAA)](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2019/882/oj)
- [Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)](https://www.ada.gov/law-and-regs/ada/)
