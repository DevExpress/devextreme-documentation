In this step-by-step tutorial, you will build a simple task list application with a progress bar. By the end, you will have the following application:

The application includes two DevExtreme components: [DataGrid](/Documentation/Guide/UI_Components/DataGrid/Overview/) and [ProgressBar](/Documentation/Guide/UI_Components/ProgressBar/Overview/). The DataGrid displays tasks, and you can mark them as complete or incomplete. The ProgressBar above the DataGrid indicates task completion progress.
