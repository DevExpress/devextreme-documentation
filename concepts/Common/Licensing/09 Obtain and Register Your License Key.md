Log in to the [DevExpress Download Manager](https://www.devexpress.com/ClientCenter/DownloadManager/) to obtain your DevExpress license key.

![Download Your DevExpress License Key](/images/Common/download-your-license-key.png)

Register the key on your machine using one of the methods described in the [License Key for DevExpress Products](https://docs.devexpress.com/GeneralInformation/405494/trial-register/set-up-your-dev-express-license-key#manual-registration-obtain-and-register-your-key) article.

[note] Every developer on your team must obtain and use their own key. Your DevExpress account manager should [Assign Licenses](https://www.devexpress.com/ClientCenter/LicenseManager) to developers.
